Table Etiquette and Player Expectations

The goal at the table is for everyone to have fun and feel comfortable participating.

Side conversations and discussion are fine as long as they are not disruptive. Engagement is more important than strict silence, and collaborative moments are encouraged.

Phones and devices are welcome at the table and are expected to be used primarily for character sheets, rules reference, and notes. If an important call or message comes in, it is completely fine to step away briefly to take it.

Please be mindful of shared spotlight time and give others space to contribute. If something is unclear or not working for you, speak up. This is a cooperative game, and we will adjust as needed.

Feedback

I want you all to feel that you can provide feedback, however I would like to keep it primarily out of session as I would like to make this as immersive a campaign as I can muster for you all, this can be done through discussion at the beginning or end of a session, or via text.
