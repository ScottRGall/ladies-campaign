Session 3 - Looking to play both sides

Session 3 Image Log

Where You Left Off

You made it back to the Hearthleaf after a long road, a messy fight, and a night that ended in two very different kinds of company.

What Happened

The morning began with planning.

Before Kerra arrived at eleven, you used the time at the Hearthleaf to align on what you would ask — and what you would not. Ellyn came up. Nothing was decided.

At the meeting, Kerra did not offer much.

You asked about power in Halvyr — the King, the council, the shape of things. Answers were thin. The brick and Hollis didn't come up.

You moved on quickly:

work for her.

You accepted a caravan escort from Brindlemark without asking any questions other than the tactical of the job.

Outbound was quiet. Camp was quiet with a few small critters nearby.

At Brindlemark, Harlan was cordial, the brewmaster of Brindlemark. You left with cargo and a clearer sense of what that town is for in the trade around Halvyr.

On the return road, with Lysa it went bad fast.

Rogues hit the caravan. They wanted specific casks — Gilded Stag–marked goods — not the whole load. You chose fight over talk or deal.

Steel, fire, and panic:

  • Athywn’s fire bolt missed, hit a tree, and lit the roadside up.
  • One cart caught fire Celienne helped douse the flames back with her waterskin; Pip finished the job in a way nobody at this table will ever un-see.
  • The attackers tried to silence witnesses. You kept one alive anyway. Someone else got away.
  • Afterward Athywn spent about an hour putting out her fire with the rest of you — buckets, magic, and patience.
  • Back on the road to Halvyr
  • Presumably the escaped rogue took another shot at your captive nailing him in the face, he went down hard, but Elodie acted quickly with a heal for him and you loaded him into the cart under some cargo.
  • You rushed back to the city at that point.

Guards were posted in shifts with your prisoner, and in the morning you delivered the captive to Kerra and the city guard, she was a bit disappointed that you would be late delivering the cargo but understood why and was pleased that you'd brought one of the assailants back alive.

You finished the delivery to the storage yards, and were paid 150 gold through Rowan.

That same evening — instead of resting on the job — you went to the Iron Bend.

Marrick took a shine to Celienne. And after an eventful game of Rollies and some "Non Invasive Pleasure", he agreed to set up a meeting with Neris Holt.

Then you headed back to the Hearthleaf for the evening feeling safer there.

What You Learned

Kerra might be a path upward. You said out loud that you do not trust her yet.

The road is being tested. Someone is willing to burn cargo, kill witnesses, and disappear when pressed.

You can work for stability and still knock on the underworld’s door — for now.

Playing both sides has a shelf life. You can feel it.

Where You Stand Now

You’re back at the Hearthleaf for the night.

  • Kerra has your prisoner.
  • One attacker is still out there.
  • Marrick owes you an introduction to Neris.
  • Ellyn is still with Kerra. You haven’t acted on that yet.

Tomorrow’s questions are louder than yesterday’s:

Who was behind this caravan hit?

What will Neris have to say if she'll meet us at all?

How long can you keep one foot in each camp?
